Future Aspirations

My first year at Gonzaga, I had wanted to become a school counselor, since then, I have moved from wanting to become a psychologist, math teacher, and now a marketing analyist! Something interesting about me is that I am a pretty fickle person, good or bad, it makes me an aspirational person; I might be an analyist in the future, but I could change my mind again.

If possible, I would love a job that is flexible in nature and in work schedule.
